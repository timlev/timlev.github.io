Read Hanna's Story

Introduction




 It is difficult to raise a family in a new country.



 This woman came to the United States with her children and grandchildren.



 She takes care of her granddaughter.



 They like to read together.




   Hello, my name is Hanna.




   I am from Ethiopia.




    My family came to the United States eight years ago.




    My granddaughter is 4 years old.




    She likes to read books.




    She wants me to read to her.




    I have trouble reading in English.




    We don't have many children's books in Amharic.




    Sometimes, I tell her stories from my country.




    I teach her about Ethiopia.




    I want her to know about Ethiopia.



    Sometimes, she makes pictures for the story.




    Sometimes, my granddaughter reads to me.




    I'm proud of my granddaughter.

Conclusion




  It's important to spend time with children every day.



 Reading together is good.



 Telling stories is good.



 Making pictures is good.



 Talking together is good.



 All of these things help children learn and grow.
